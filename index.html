About TOONAV

TOONAV is the annual techno-cultural fest of GL Bajaj Group of Institutions, Mathura. It brings together students from across the region to showcase their talents in technical, cultural, literary, and sports events. Join us for an unforgettable experience of innovation, creativity, and celebration.

We bring together brands and college students to create unforgettable experiences. Our varied range of events will give an exposure to the student fraternity to explore their talent and get rewarded just for being students, while the vendors & brands are able to market effectively to thousands of college students. Our goal is to create unique experiences for both students and vendors. The different activities in this fest will provide the participants to unleash their talent to a large group of audience and will make their spirits high and rejuvenating and will be an unforgettable experience for all. The fest will provide them with various opportunities that will help towards their overall development and give them a great exposure and make them learn to collaborate, organize work in teams and go for out of the box solutions.
